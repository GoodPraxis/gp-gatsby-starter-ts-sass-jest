I Love You Infinity is an infinite audio experiment based on the unique way one autistic child expresses his love.

The project launched on Global Autism Awareness Week 2020 to challenge damaging myths around autism and lack of emotion, and will continue to play forever.

Share Infinite Love

Share this website to help raise awareness of the spectrum of ways autistic minds express and experience love. Follow on Instagram and submit stories of love. Exhibit this audio experiment. Support the National Autistic Society.
